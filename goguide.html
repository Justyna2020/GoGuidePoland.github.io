General Information:

The population of Poland, currently 38.4 million people.
The cities are mostly small or medium sized.

43 cities have populations of more than 100,000 inhabitants.
Warsaw, the capital and Poland's largest metropolitan area, has a population of 1.7 million people.
The five other largest cities are: Łódź, Kraków, Wrocław, Poznań and Gdańsk.
The population of Polish communities abroad is estimated at 12 million
, with the largest living in the USA (5.6 mn.), CIS (2.5 mn.), France (1 mn.), Germany (800,000), Canada (790,000), Brazil (200,000), Australia (150,000), and the UK (140,000).
